Hello!

My name is Abby Burns, a Senior Journalism major and Interactive Multimedia minor at The College of New Jersey.

Welcome to my portfolio website, where all of my writing clips can be found. I am Co-Editor-in-Chief of TCNJ's chapter of Her Campus, an online magazine for college-aged women, and am a content writer for TCNJ's School of Engineering website. I also have included writing samples from my internship at Providence Media, as well as some samples from journalism classes I have taken at TCNJ, including Magazine Writing and Writing for Interactive Media. As an Interactive Multimedia minor, I created this portfolio website on my own from html and css to showcase my website building skills.
